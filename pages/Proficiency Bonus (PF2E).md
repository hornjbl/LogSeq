alias:: Proficiency Bonus

- A system that measures a character's aptitude at a specific task or quality.
- | **Proficiency Rank** | **Proficiency Bonus** |
  | Untrained | 0 |
  | Trained | Your level + 2 |
  | Expert | Your level + 4 |
  | Master | Your level + 6 |
  | Legendary | Your level + 8 |
